Movies

My favourite film of all time is Jim Henson's Labyrinth (1986).

Labyrinth is a film about Sarah, a young girl who is left by her father and step-mother to look after her baby brother Toby for an evening.

She inavertantly summons Jareth, the Goblin King, to take Toby away.

Sarah must navigate Jareth's labryinth to resuce Toby and bring him home safe and sound.

Labyrinth is a coming-of-age fantasy film about bravery, maturity, masculinity, and the role that arts and media can play in helping us through difficult times in our lives.

My least favourite film is The Imitation Game.
